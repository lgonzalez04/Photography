A LITTLE ABOUT ME

Introduction

I would like to take a second to thank you for considering me to be your photographer. My name is Luis Gonzalez and I grew up in Panama City, Panama and now I call Louisville Kentucky home. I am a regular photographer who absolutely loves his job and every part of it. I appreciate people I meet, the places I go and the time I spend in every shoot.
Aside from taking pictures, another passion of mine is developing websites. I specialise in creating beautiful, professional and usable websites using HTML, CSS, and JAVASCRIPT. I love being able to manage every part of the website and been able to design and build websites from scratch.
How did I get to the U.S? A beautiful lady went to Panama for a study abroad and she brought a souvenier with her (I was that souvineer).
Are you wondering why I have a shoebox and a Lamb? If you'd like to hear my shoebox story follow the link: samaritanspurse.org
